Put me on during a rainy day in a room that you occaisionally walk by. Every now and then, pop in and see what has changed, sip your coffee and leave. There's nothing to consider with any of this, there's no pretense. Turn your computer into something passive for a while. You can use different seed words to change the environment, but by no means should you deliberate on these strings.

This is just a screensaver. It's just a painting.

There's nothing gamified in this which you can min-max, there are no ads, there is no timeframe. This website does not need your participation in any way shape or form. This website does not take your data, there is no ulterior motive. There is no way that you can use this website improperly. Once you press 'enter' some light music will come on which you can toggle off and it will maximize to a full screen.

Then you can walk away.
